A Rich Buisness Environment

Utah consistently makes top rankings for business, economics and more. Bankrate.com ranks Utah as the #2 state form small businesses in 2024.

Provo, Utah boast a robust business environment. Not only is the overall business climate friendly, but the city has serveral hubs of activity that offer unique opportunities for new businesses.

From Easy Bay, right off 1-15, to downtown, to the northern Shops at the Riverwoods near the mouth of the canyon, to potential partnerships and incoming talent from Universities, you'll find Provo to be a unique and diverse place to set up your business. And don't foget to visit the Provo Pioneer Villiage to see where it all started.
